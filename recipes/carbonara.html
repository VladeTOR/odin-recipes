Carbonara

Description

In this article i will try to guide you to make one of the most famous Italian dish.

The pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 100gr pancetta
  • 50ggr pecorino cheese
  • 50gr parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350gr spaghetti
  • 2 plump garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • sea salt and freshly ground black papper

Steps:

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil
  2. Finely chop the 100g pancetta, having first removed any rind.Finaly grate 50g pecorino cheese and 50g parmesan and mix them together
