Gyros

Description

Make a Greek gyro sandwich at home with this recipe thet i absolutly love!

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp dried origano
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp dried marjoran
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp dried rosemary
  • 1 tbsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 12 tbsp hummus
  • 12 pita bread rounds
  • 1 small head lattuce, shredded
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 large red onion, sliced
  • 6 ounces crumbled feta cheese
  • 24 tbsp tzatziki sauce

Steps:

  1. Place onion in a food processor and finely chop. Transfer onion to a piace cheesecloth and squeeze out the liquid. Place onion in a large bowl.
  2. Mix lamb, beef, garlic, oregano, cumin, marjoram, thyme, rosemary, b;ack papper, and salt with the onion using your hands until well mixed. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigirate until flawors blend, about 2 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 320 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  4. Place meat mixture in a food processor and pulse until fined chopped and tacky, about 1 minute. Pack meat mixture into a 7x4-inch loaf pan, ensuring there are no air pockets. Place the loaf pa into a roasting pan and pour enough boiling wateraround the loaf pan to reach halfway up the sides for a water bath.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, 45 to 60 minutes. An instant-read thermometar inserted into the center should real at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C). Pour off any accumulated fat and cool slightly.
  6. Thinly slice the cooked gyro meat loaf.
  7. Spread 1 tbsp hummus onto each pita bread. The top each with some of the gyro meat slices, shredded lattuce, tomato slices, red onion slices, feta cheese crumbles and 2 tbsp tzatziki sauce to complete each sandwich.
